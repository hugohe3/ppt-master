# Sponsor PPT Master

English | [中文](SPONSORING_CN.md)

PPT Master is a free, open-source workflow for turning documents or topics into native, editable PowerPoint decks. It is independently maintained and remains provider-agnostic. Sponsorship helps fund maintenance, compatibility work, examples, documentation, and the infrastructure used by the project.

Sponsorship is considered selectively. A partnership must be useful to PPT Master users and must not compromise the project's technical independence or user trust.

## Partnership Placements

### Lead Sponsor

A prominent placement for one primary partner whose product has a strong fit with PPT Master's users.

Potential placement includes:

- The expanded sponsor block near the top of `README.md` and `README_CN.md`.
- The lead position in [`skills/ppt-master/SPONSORS.md`](skills/ppt-master/SPONSORS.md), which travels with standalone Skill distributions.
- A relevant, clearly disclosed mention at an existing user decision point.

### Service Partner

For services that solve a real need in the PPT Master workflow, such as agent or model access, AI image generation, narration, cloud infrastructure, or hosted execution.

Potential placement includes:

- A listing in the repository and standalone Skill sponsor pages.
- A context-specific mention in existing setup or troubleshooting documentation.
- A dedicated referral link, promotion code, or user benefit supplied by the partner.

### Technical Integration Partner

For providers whose API or hosted service is genuinely supported by an existing PPT Master integration boundary.

Technical integration requires compatibility review and must remain optional. Sponsorship alone does not make a provider a default, bypass validation, or justify provider-specific behavior inside the presentation workflow.

### Supporting Sponsor

A compact logo and link placement for organizations that support ongoing maintenance without requiring a workflow integration.

## Partnership Requirements

Partners should provide:

- An accurate product description and approved brand assets.
- A stable destination URL, preferably with a trackable referral parameter.
- A concrete benefit for PPT Master users when applicable.
- A current contact for operational or content updates.
- Prompt notice when pricing, availability, branding, or promotional terms change.

All promotional claims remain subject to review. Expired, misleading, unavailable, or technically incompatible offers may be corrected or removed.

## Project Boundaries

Sponsorship does not include:

- Influence over the project roadmap or quality gates.
- Access to user files, prompts, generated presentations, or usage data.
- Advertising inside exported PPTX files.
- Automatic selection of a sponsor's service without user choice.
- Custom development, private deployment, or a commercial support SLA.
- An endorsement beyond the exact placement and wording agreed for the partnership.
